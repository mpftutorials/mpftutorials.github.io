MilPDS Updates


How to access milPDS:
  1. Get onto the milPDS website
  2. Sign in with EMAIL certificate, every time it asks (it can ask multiple times).
Adding or updating Dependents and Family Members

1 / 8
double click Customer Service
2 / 8
Type in the SSN of the sponsor, then Find (Alt+I)
3 / 8
Profile will pop up. Go to Dependents
4 / 8
Currently existing dependents will show up first. If not, it will be blank.
5 / 8
Press the down key to start a new dependent.
6 / 8
After typing in the name, SSN (or temp ID), birthday, sex, describe the relationship. For new children, "From" will be their birthday, and for new spouses it is the date of marriage.
7 / 8
Scroll to the right and uncheck "Create Mirror" first, check "Dependent", then press the blank bar at the end.
8 / 8
Fill in the options. For non-military spouses, leave Joint Spouse questions blank. Then, save by clicking the yellow disk at the top tab.

Updating citizenship


  1. Go to Scott AFB (SF) > Customer Service > Customer support > Customer Service > Personal Data > Additional Info
  2. Press [F11] to activate search box, type in SSN, then Ctrl+[F11], Alt+A
  3. Check Details, then change the value in Citizenship
