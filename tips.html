Welcome to Customer Support.
Your life will not be easy.
There is always something going on, and going wrong. Something is always broken, and we work with what we have. By the time we open a 7:30, a horde of customers fill up the seats. After waiting for hours, they begin coming to the front desk with pitchfork and fire.

Yes, this place is a shark tank. A lot of our tools don't work. HOWEVER, you can make your life easier and the job go faster by learning a few simple tricks, because our computers should help us, not slow us down.

The shortcuts written about here can be used on both the laptops and the RAPIDS terminals. Many of us know this, but this tutorial was made for the base common denominator - the person who may have not used computers that much before.

Keyboard Shortcuts



Keys What it does: Helpful for:
[Ctrl] + F Find looking up the AFI guide (a PDF file) on the RAPIDS computer.
Once you open up the AFI guide, you can quickly find what you're looking for by using Ctrl+F and typing in your topic.
[Tab] skip to the next tab skipping over to the next tabs without having to hover the mouse around
[Alt] + [Tab] switch between different windows Switch tasks/windows/browsers without having to use your mouse to click on the tabs
