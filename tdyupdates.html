TDY Updates

  1. Press the F11 Key. Enter member’s name (Last, First) followed by “%” or enter the SSN with dashes. Press Ctrl+F11, columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
  2. Click TDY ASSIGNMENT.
  3. Click Insert to create new TDY.
  4. Place cursor in “TDY Type” field and click LOV button. Select the appropriate TDY type.
  5. Place cursor in the “Date Depart Current” field, then type in date DD-MON-YY.
  6. Place cursor in “EST TOUR LENGTH” field. Input # of days mbr will be TDY. Once you input the # of days, the EXPIRE DATE field will automatically update. If not go to Step 7.
  7. Place cursor in “EXPIRE DATE” field. Input the date TDY expires.
  8. Place cursor in “CNTRY” field and click LOV button. Select the appropriate TDY location.
  9. Click Save.
  10. Select “DATE RET CURR” field. Enter date mbr returned from TDY.
  11. Click Save. Once the TDY is saved, it’ll disappear. Click View History to to verify that TDY has been added to record.
