CAFSC Updates

Ensure you are checking for CAFSC. DAFSC is something that is only done by CSS.
  1. Go to milPDS: Press Ctrl+L, then type in %sei. Select whether enlisted/officer AFSC/SEI
  2. Find the member: Press the [F11] on keyboard. Enter the SSN with dashes, then Ctrl+[F11]. Columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
    **If you don't have social, you can also find the member with the % sign at the end.
    Example: To look for "SNUFFY, BILLY", type something like "SNUFFY, B%" in the name box.
  3. Click the Duty Information button.
  4. Click Duty Info button
  5. Click ASGNMT DUTY CONTROL
  6. Click in the Details field
  7. Select then copy current AFSC in “CAFSC” field, then paste it in the “CAFSC Previous” field.
  8. Update the new CAFSC in the CAFSC field.
  9. Update the CAFSC effective date.
  10. Click Ok and save.
