Duty Title Updates

The duty title will describe the actual job and level of responsibility of the individual.

The CSS of the member whose duty title needs an update will send you a form AF2096 properly filled out. Ensure that the form has the following things:
DAFSC
EFFECTIVE DATE
OFFICE SYMBOL
DUTY PH
POSITION NO.
DUTY TITLE
and has signatures of everyone
  1. Press the F11 Key. Enter the SSN with dashes. Press Ctrl+F11, Columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
  2. Click the Duty Information button.
  3. Click the Alter Effective Date Calendar icon on toolbar. Update the effective date listed. Click OK and save the transaction.
  4. Delete the Organization. Click the LOV button “…”.
    Type first 4 letters or full PASCODE followed by %. Click Ok. Select PASCODE. Click OK and Save.
  5. Place cursor in the “Job” field. Delete AFSC information. Click LOV button.
    Type new DAFSC (ex. -3S071) and click OK.
  6. Select the appropriate DAFSC and click OK.
  7. A pop up box will appear, then Click the “Update” button.
  8. Place the cursor in the “Position” field. Click the LOV button. Type partial/full pos # or scroll down to find pos #, select pos # and click OK.
  9. Select the appropriate pos #, click OK. Select Ok, if pop up box appears and click save.
  10. Click the Duty Information button.
  11. Click “ASGNMT DUTY” field.
  12. Click to the left of the first blank line to open field. Click blank highlighted field. A pop up box will appear.
  13. Update the Duty Start Date, DAFSC, Duty Cmd Level, Duty title.
  14. Click Ok and save icon on the toolbar
