Duty Title Updates

The duty title will describe the actual job and level of responsibility of the individual.

The CSS of the member whose duty title needs an update will send you a form AF2096 properly filled out. Ensure that the form has the following things:
DAFSC
EFFECTIVE DATE
OFFICE SYMBOL
DUTY PH
POSITION NO.
DUTY TITLE
and has signatures of everyone
  1. Go to milPDS: Press Ctrl+L, then type in %duty info and UT ASSIGN FOLDER should pop up.
  2. Find the member: Press the [F11] on keyboard. Enter the SSN with dashes, then Ctrl+[F11]. Columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
    **If you don't have social, you can also find the member with the % sign at the end.
    Example: To look for "SNUFFY, BILLY", type something like "SNUFFY, B%" in the name box.
  3. Click the Duty Information button.
  4. Click the Alter Effective Date Calendar icon on toolbar. Update the effective date listed. Click OK and save the transaction.
  5. Delete the Organization. Click the LOV button “…”.
    Type first 4 letters or full PASCODE followed by %. Click Ok. Select PASCODE. Click OK and Save.
  6. Place cursor in the “Job” field. Delete AFSC information. Click LOV button.
    Type new DAFSC (ex. -3S071) and click OK.
  7. Select the appropriate DAFSC and click OK.
  8. A pop up box will appear, then Click the “Update” button.
  9. Place the cursor in the “Position” field. Click the LOV button. Type partial/full pos # or scroll down to find pos #, select pos # and click OK.
  10. Select the appropriate pos #, click OK. Select Ok, if pop up box appears and click save.
  11. Click the Duty Information button.
  12. Click “ASGNMT DUTY” field.
  13. Click to the left of the first blank line to open field. Click blank highlighted field. A pop up box will appear.
  14. Update the Duty Start Date, DAFSC, Duty Cmd Level, Duty title.
  15. Click Ok and save icon on the toolbar

Click on the Duty Information button.

Click the Alter Effective Date Calendar icon on the toolbar, Update the Effective Date once the Alter Effective Date window appears, Click OK, Click Save.
Note: Always alter the effective date when updating or correcting (Organization, Job AFSC, and Position number) unless the update action is effective today.
4 / 9


When making a correction (when the current effective date is correct, however, the Organization, Job or Position number is invalid) always alter the effective date to one day after the current effective date.
EXAMPLE: SSgt Doe’s current effective date is 1-Nov-17, you would alter effective date to 2-Nov-17 when updating a correction.
The Alter Effective Date window will populate with the new info.
5 / 9


Delete the Organization. Click the List of Values button “…”. Type the first 4 letters of the PAS code followed by a percent sign (%) and Click the OK button.
Select the appropriate PASCODE, then click OK button and save.
**Only click the correction button when correcting the current assignment effective date.
6 / 9


Place the cursor in the Jobs field. Delete information Click the LOV button. Type the new DAFSC (-3S071) and click OK.
Select the appropriate DAFSC, then click OK.
Click Update button.
**This will only appear if you did not change the organization (PAS code).
**DAFSC must match position #, per the UMPR. If the DAFSC doesn’t match, then the position number will not populate.
7 / 9


Place the cursor in the Position field. Click the LOV button. Type the partial/full position # or scroll to find position #, select position # and click OK.
Select the appropriate position number, then click OK. Select update, if, pop up box appears and save.
Click the Duty Information button.
8 / 9


Click Assignment Duty field.
Click to the left of the first blank line to open field. Click blank highlighted field. A pop up box will appear.
Only update the current duty info field if you’re correcting erroneous data.
9 / 9


Update the Duty Start Date. Update the DAFSC. Update the Duty Cmd Level. Update the Duty Title.
Click OK and save icon on the toolbar.

Office Symbol Update

  1. Go to milPDS: Press Ctrl+L, then type in %customer and select Customer Service
  2. Find the member: Enter the SSN with dashes, or type in their last name, first name. Columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
    **If you don't have social, you can also find the member with the % sign at the end.
    Example: To look for "SNUFFY, BILLY", type something like "SNUFFY, B%" in the name box.
  3. Select Alter Effective Date calendar icon at the top bar (looks like little calendar with "31" on it).
    Update the Effective Date according to the AF2096 you were provided.
  4. Click the Office Details tab
  5. The Office field will have the old office symbol. Replace it it with new Office Symbol
  6. Save
