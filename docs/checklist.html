DECORATION REVIEW CHECKLIST

  • Make sure that the digital signature is going to be same individual on the signature block.
  • Signature block must be:
    NAME, RANK, BRANCH OF SERVICE DUTY TITLE, ASSIGNED UNIT

  • Check the number of citations per AFI 3.4.8.:
    MSM, AM, AAM, and AFCM citations: 14 lines maximum
    AFAM citations: 12 lines maximum

  • Check the name on top, correct spelling, accurate dates
  • First sentence:
    Full name and rank: (ex. Technical Sergeant John D. Doe distinguished himself by…)
    Full location: (ex. ...375th Air Mobility Wing, Scott Air Force Base, Illinois.)

  • After first sentence, name may be shortened “ex. During this period, Sergeant Doe…”
  • Check spelling
  • Check appropriate punctuations
  • No abbreviations
  • All titles, awards, etc. are appropriately capitalized
  • When talking about money can either be written as: “$6 million” or “6 million dollars” NOT= “$6 million dollars”
  • Cannot use ‘%’ sign. Must actually spell out “percent”
  • Make sure that sentences make sense
  • Make sure they are using correct ‘Condition #’ as intended.
  • Condition Numbers:
    0:ACT OF COURAGE
    1:RET
    2:SEP
    3:POST/DEATH
    4:ACHIEVEMENT
    5:HEROISM
    6:PCS
    7:PCA
    8:EXTD TOUR
