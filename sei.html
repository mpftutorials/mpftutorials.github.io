Special Experience Identifier (SEI) updates

SEIs are established to identify special experience and training not not otherwise identified in MilPDS

SEI Update for Enlisted

  1. Go to milPDS: Press Ctrl+L, then type in %sei and select the Enlisted AFSC/SEI
  2. Press the F11 Key. Enter member’s name (Last, First) followed by “%” or enter the SSN with dashes. Press Ctrl+F11, columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
  3. Click Enlisted SEIs.
  4. Click in the AFSC field, then Click the LOV button “…”. A pop up box will appear.
  5. Select the applicable AFSC for the awarded SEI and select ok.
  6. Place cursor in the “SEI code” field, then click LOV button.
  7. Select SEI and click save.

SEI Update for Officers

  1. Go to milPDS: Press Ctrl+L, then type in %sei and select the Officer AFSC/SEI
  2. Press the F11 Key. Enter member’s name (Last, First) followed by “%” or enter the SSN with dashes. Press Ctrl+F11, columns will populate with the member’s information. Click on member’s name.
  3. Click Officer SEI.
  4. Click in the Details field. A pop up box will appear.
  5. Click the LOV button “…”, then Click Ok. A pop up box will appear.
  6. Place cursor in the “Activity Code” field, then click LOV button.
    Select appropriate code.
  7. Cursor will move to Experience Set” field. Click LOV button. Type % and hit enter.
    Select appropriate code and click ok. Close window. Click Save.
